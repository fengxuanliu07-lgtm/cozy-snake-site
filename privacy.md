# Privacy Policy for Cozy Snake

**Last updated: November 16, 2026**

Cozy Snake ("the app") is developed by Jack Liu. This privacy policy explains how the app handles information.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

Cozy Snake is designed as a quiet, offline experience. The app does not:

- Collect personal data
- Require accounts or sign-ins
- Track user location
- Use third-party analytics
- Show advertisements
- Include third-party SDKs that collect data
- Transmit any user data to external servers

## Information Stored on Your Device

Cozy Snake uses your device's local storage to remember your game progress, including:

- Your current level and score
- Unlocked skins, foods, boards, and trails
- Daily streak count
- Game preferences (language, sound, mode)
- Custom snake name (if set)

This data stays entirely on your device. It is never transmitted to us or any third party. If you delete the app, this data is removed from your device.

## Notifications

The app may schedule local notifications on your device to remind you about daily streaks, only if you explicitly enable reminders in the app's menu. These notifications are scheduled locally on your device through Apple's notification system. We do not send any push notifications from a server, and we have no access to your notification history.

You can disable reminders at any time from the app's menu, or in iOS Settings → Notifications → Cozy Snake.

## Children's Privacy

Cozy Snake is rated 4+ and is appropriate for all ages, but is not specifically directed at children under 13. Because we collect no personal information at all, the app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
